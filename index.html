How to Read
This dashboard provides a quick view of compliance performance across regions and companies, along with a central place for status updates and explanations for expired permits. Permits are pulled daily from Origami to provide an accurate list of expired and soon-to-expire permits. Origami remains the system of record. Properties should add notes only when a permit cannot yet be updated in Origami, to explain the delay or request help. Click any region in Standings below to view its properties and permits.
